Welcome

Brothers and Friends,
 
Welcome to the “Landmark of the Valley” website. You are invited to browse through the information to learn who we are and what we do.

Our Valley extends from Herington, Kansas north to Nebraska and west to Colorado. Our Rite Care Centers are located at Hays, Kansas, where the Herndon Learning and Speech Center is a part of the Fort Hays State University campus, and Manhattan, Kansas, on the Kansas State University campus.
 
Sincerely and Respectfully,
John Reynolds 33°
